Mashed potatoes with cheese then add some finely chopped onion,tomatoes,chilli,bell corn and capsicums.Mix it well.
And then add some chedder cheese.And again mix it,addsome chilli flakes and garam masala,dahnia masal,jeera powder and some spoons of mayonise.And mix it again.
Add some salt at last.The mix again. Then make some small balls of the mixture.Then take some cornflour and breadcrums.First roll the balls in the breadcrums and then in cornflour.
And the deep fry in the air frier.And then after minutes take it out and server it hot with mayonies,mustered sauce , chillie sauce or tomatoe sauce.
